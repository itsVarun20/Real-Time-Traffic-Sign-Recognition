App Usage Guide:


1. Once you launch the Traffic Sign Detection App.
2. Grant the necessary camera permissions when prompted.
3. The camera feed will be displayed on the screen.
4. Hold a traffic sign within the view of the camera.
5. The app will analyze the traffic sign in real-time.
6. The corresponding sign name and accuracy will be displayed on the screen.
7. Repeat the process with different traffic signs to explore the app's accuracy.
8. To return, click the home button on top right.
